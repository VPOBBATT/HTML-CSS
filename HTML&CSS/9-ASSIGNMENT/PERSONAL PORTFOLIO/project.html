Project

1.Feedback System

Students often get the opportunity to listen to the eminent professors, industry
specialists, and experts from different sectors. The guest lectures organised at MGM
college were extremely helpful in deepening the understanding of the current
technology trends. The loop is closed by giving feedback to the guests in order them to
know how useful their knowledge sharing was and how important it was for the students to
interact with them. Recognizing the importance of digital feedback, Vijaya Lakshmi
developed an application using HTMl,CSS,PHp and MySQL.
